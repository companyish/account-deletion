Delete Your Account

We respect your right to control your data. If you'd like to delete your Foodprints account and all associated data, here's how.

How to request deletion

  1. 1
    Send us an email Email contact@companyish.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request".
  2. 2
    Include your registered phone number This is the phone number you used to sign up for Foodprints. We need it to identify your account.
  3. 3
    We'll confirm and delete We'll verify your identity, permanently delete all your data, and send you a confirmation email.

What gets deleted

  • Your account and authentication credentials
  • Profile information (name, city, avatar)
  • All meal logs, ratings, and notes
  • Dish and restaurant history
  • Uploaded photos (meal photos, bill scans, profile picture)
  • All locally stored data on your device

Deletion timeline

Your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days of your request. During this period, your account will be deactivated and inaccessible. Once deletion is complete, this action cannot be undone.

Questions about your data or privacy? Contact us at contact@companyish.com.
